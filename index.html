Agricultural area iLand that is currently used for farming or could be used for farming with the resources available on the land.

Permanent cropland iLand that is cultivated with crops that are left in the ground for multiple years, and do not need to be replanted after each harvest.
ha
Permanent pasture iLand that has been used for five or more years to grow forage, such as grasses, herbs, legumes, or wildflowers, and has not been used for other crops during that time.

ha

Synthetic inputs

All questions are required to proceed.

Integrated nutrient management (C5)

Legal compliance
Meet all requirements under the Farming Rules for Water (and related regulations).
Planning
Have a nutrient management plan in place (or formally planned) for 2026/27.
Evidence
Complete soil tests at least once every 4 years on any land receiving nutrient inputs (from 2026).

Integrated pest management (C6)

Legal compliance
Comply with the Sustainable Use of Pesticides legislation.
Good practice at handling
Use drip trays when filling; dispose of caps/seals responsibly.
Planning
Develop, maintain, and use an effective written IPM plan during 2026/27.
